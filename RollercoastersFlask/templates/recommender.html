About the developer

My name is Lars Bakker, and welcome to the Roller Coaster Recommender page. I completed a comprehensive, self-developed IT program.

This taught me the basic principles of ethical hacking, advanced mathematics, and web development.

I completed these programs through Harvard CS50, IT Management Group and individual courses from Delft University of Technology EDX.

I'm a fan of rollercoasters and Europa Park, so I built a web application in Flask that recommends the most suitable Europa Park coaster based on questions about your height and roller coaster preferences.

Europa Park has a total of 14 roller coasters and it's impossible to do them all in one visit, so it's always useful to know which ones you shouldn't miss.

Once the questionnaire is completed, the most recommended roller coaster will appear, along with its specifications and a "POV" onride video, Enjoy!

About the experience ratings of the roller coasters

The coaster experience ratings in this web app are general indicators and can vary significantly from person to person.

They are based on the average roller coaster rider and should not be interpreted as medical or professional advice.

Individuals with sensitive stomachs, balance issues, heart conditions, or other health concerns should always exercise caution when riding roller coasters.

Your personal experience, comfort level, and riding history also play an important role.

For example, an experienced young adult may find Voltron to be an exceptional ride, while a 9 year old on their first roller coaster may find it too intense.

All information provided in this web app is intended solely for educational and entertainment purposes.

When visiting Europa_Park, always follow the official safety instructions and signage at each attraction.
