Coaster Information

Voltron is Europa Park's most intense roller coaster.

Blue Fire is a looping coaster for anyone who wants to try something thrilling, but Voltron is for the true coaster hunters.

It features several impressive inversions.

Don't be shy about the screaming in this masterpiece.

An advice: If you have sensitive ears, I'd recommend earplugs for the waiting area of Voltron.

The sound effects in the waiting area are a bit overwhelming.
