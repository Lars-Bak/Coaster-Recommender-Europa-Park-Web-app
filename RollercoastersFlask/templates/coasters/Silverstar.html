Coaster Information

With a top speed of 130 km/h, the Silverstar is the fastest and tallest roller coaster at Europa Park.

You can choose your seat on the Silverstar, so choose the front row for the best view and the most wind in your face for a greater sense of speed.

Choose the back row for more airtime. It's up to you and one more tip: swing your arms in the air for even more excitement and airtime.
